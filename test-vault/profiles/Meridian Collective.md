# Meridian Collective

## Organization Profile
- **Type:** Non-state threat actor
- **Leader:** Unknown (suspected: [[Dr. Ilsa Venn]])
- **Structure:** Cell-based, 4-6 known cells across Europe and Asia
- **Funding:** Shell companies, crypto, stolen IP licensing
- **Key Personnel:** [[Dr. Ilsa Venn]], [[Fen Haruki]], [[Yusuf Qadir]]

## Threat Assessment
Meridian is not ideological — they sell disruption as a service. Their clients
include rogue states and corporate actors who benefit from destabilized regions.
Project Halcyon is their most ambitious undertaking to date.
