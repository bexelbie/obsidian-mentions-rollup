# [[Operation Brass Compass]]

Major breakthrough. The tracker on Abacus led Swiss station to a
private vault in Zurich. [[Emeka Sato]] coordinated with Swiss
intelligence to get a warrant.

Contents: financial records linking [[Meridian Collective]] to
fourteen shell companies across seven countries. Total assets:
estimated €340 million.

Swiss authorities are preparing to freeze the accounts. Once they do,
Meridian's operational funding gets cut off. This could cripple them.

[[Margaux Devereaux]] wants to time the financial strike with the
Tanglewood endgame for maximum impact.
